General Tips for Solo Play

📜
Guidelines, not rules. The tables and generators are tools to spark your imagination. Override any result that doesn't fit your story.
🎭
Be your character. React to the world as your character would. Let their personality shape which hooks you follow and which you ignore.
⚡
Keep the flow moving. Don't agonize over every roll. If a result doesn't make sense, reroll or interpret creatively and move on.
🔄
Fail forward. When things go wrong, let the complications drive the story. A failed check isn't the end — it's a twist.
📝
Take notes. Jot down NPC names, settlement details, and quest threads. Your notes become the world's memory.
🎲
Use the Oracle. When you're not sure what happens next, ask the Oracle a yes/no question. It's always available in the sidebar.

Luck

Luck points let you bend fate when it matters most. Characters start with 3 Luck and gain 1 whenever they roll doubles during exploration.

Cost Effect
1 LuckReroll any single die
1 LuckAdd or remove a detail from the scene
2 LuckAuto-succeed a check (no roll needed)
2 LuckEscape a deadly situation
3 LuckIntroduce a major narrative twist in your favor
